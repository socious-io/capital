Who We Are

Socious Capital is an investment management company focused on early-stage opportunities in challenging geographies. We bridge the gap between institutional capital and high-impact ventures in regions eligible for Official Development Assistance (ODA).

Our Philosophy

We believe that the most significant returns come from solving the most urgent problems. Our strategy focuses on "Inclusive and Sustainable Communities" and "Decent Work".

By leveraging deep local networks and rigorous impact measurement, we identify founders—often overlooked by traditional capital—who are building the infrastructure for the next generation of emerging market growth.

Our Strategy: The Socious Fund

This vehicle is designed to meet the rigorous standards of global impact investors.

100% Emerging Market Focus

Our assets are deployed exclusively in ODA-eligible countries, targeting high-growth regions in Africa and South Asia.

Social Finance First

At least 75% of our portfolio contributes directly to solving social inequalities, providing adequate living standards, or creating decent work.

Rigorous Measurement

We utilize best-in-class frameworks, including the Theory of Change and IRIS+, to monitor ESG risks and quantify positive outcomes.
